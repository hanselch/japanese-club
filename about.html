About us


We are students dedicated to understanding the language, culture, cuisine, people, politics, history and more from Japan. Students here will be understanding in a way that students can continue to use the language, and to participate not only within themselves but if not a collaborative effort between this small group. The dedication needed for this class is necessary, the reason being is that the language (arguably) is one of the most difficult languages in the world. With this motivation, we will make learning helpful not only between conversation, but also business, communication, the understanding of being bi- or trilingual, and many more.


チーム

The Team


For now, we have only one student, Hansel Chavez, given lectures about the class in language, culture, history, and more. Hansel, have been learning the language for more than three years, yet believes he is an intermediate. He considers founding this club into having more people motivating into having more than one or two languages in their memory. This not only helps them get jobs outside of the U.S. if not can be a great conversational starter when needed.



動機

Motivations


Our motivation is to help them improve, learn, and understand why we should learn Japanese. With this understanding, students can understand the best practice into not just improving their reading and writing skills, but if not, their communication, cultural, and ethical understanding. This create motivation and a future.
