Hiragana and Katakana

Hansel Chavez | 10 / 10

You will now be in the basics in hiragana and katakana, one of the fundementals in writing and reading in Japanese.


By the end of the week, you will master hirgana and katakana in no time!


Below are two PDF files you will download. Either one will show the chart of hirgana and katakana. During class, we will learn how to write them properly and speak it properly.



Hiragana_table.pdf Katakana_table.pdf


You will have homework until second block day, writing down the given example below the hiragana / katakana in seperate piece of paper.

Example:
ア == アイスクリーム
